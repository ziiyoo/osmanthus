# TIMEZONE 时区支持

> ENGLISH DESCRIPTION
> 
> The following is the support for various time zones and their corresponding UTC offset.
> 
> For example, the AST timezone has a UTC offset of -4 hours, which means that to convert the time in this timezone to the UTC timezone, you need to subtract -4 hours.
> 
> Calculation logic description
> 
> If "2023-10-10 08:00:00 ast"
> 
> So utc = "2023-10-10 08:00:00" - (-4) 
> 
> The mean utc = "2023-10-10 08:00:00" + 4
> 
> utc = "2023-10-10 12:00:00"

> 中文描述
> 
> 以下是对各个时区的支持和对应的协调时区偏移量
> 
> 例如 ast 时区相对于协调时区 utc 的偏移量是 -4 小时，即如果要将这个时区的时间转换为 utc 时区的时间需要减去 -4 小时
> 
> 计算逻辑描述
> 
> 假设 "2023-10-10 08:00:00 ast"
> 
> 那么 utc = "2023-10-10 08:00:00" - (-4)
> 
> 即 utc = "2023-10-10 08:00:00" + 4
> 
> utc = "2023-10-10 12:00:00"
